# AGENTS.md

## Mission

**Win the Nucleus Utah hackathon.** Build an AI-powered talent-to-startup matching platform that blows past LinkedIn and traditional job boards.

## Prize

$5,000

## Parameters

All hackathon requirements, judging criteria, and deliverables are in [paramaters.md](./paramaters.md). Read it before starting any work.

**Client website**: [nucleusutah.org](https://www.nucleusutah.org/) — review for design language, branding, and current UX to understand what we're improving upon.

## Quick Summary

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Problem** | Utah's innovation ecosystem lacks intelligent matching between talent (executives, operators, students, advisors) and startups (deep tech, AI, life sciences, defense, etc.) |
| **Solution** | AI-driven platform that intelligently matches talent to startups with explainable AI |
| **Key Features** | Dual-sided profiles, explainable matching, Utah-specific context, Squarespace + Affinity CRM integration |
| **Deliverables** | Working prototype/demo, AI approach explanation, Utah context integration, 2-3 example match scenarios |

## Judging Criteria (weighted)

1. **Match Quality & Intelligence** — 30%
2. **User Experience** — 40% (biggest weight — make it intuitive, clear, trustworthy)
3. **Integration** — 20% (Squarespace + Affinity CRM compatibility)
4. **Innovation & Creativity** — 10%

## Working Principles

- **UX is king** — 40% of the score. Prioritize intuitive, trust-building interfaces over clever backend tricks.
- **Explainable AI** — Users must understand *why* they were matched. No black boxes.
- **Utah-specific** — Tailor to Utah's ecosystem (U of U, BYU, USU, local sectors). This differentiates us from generic solutions.
- **Show, don't tell** — Include realistic example matches in the demo.
- **Use synthetic data** — No proprietary or sensitive personal data.
